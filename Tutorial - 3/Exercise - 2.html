Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Ingredients







Steps

Step 1

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, the baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth

Step 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pout or scoop paste the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
